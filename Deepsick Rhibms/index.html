WHAT IS RHIBMS?

It Trains Students In Health & Management

Redemption Higher Institute of Biomedical and Management Sciences (RHIBMS) with slogan "Passion
For Excellent Training" is one of the few training schools used by the Ministries of Higher Education,
Public Health and Professional training for the training of skilled personnel in various disciplines
more particularly Nurses and Laboratory Studies.


RHIBMS offers programs for HND in Midwifery, BSc in Nursing, Masters in Business
Administration(MBA) and many more programs in between.

ABOUT RHIBMS

RHIBMS BUEA is a premier institution of higher learning committed to academic excellence, innovation, and the holistic development of our students. Founded in 1995, we have grown to become one of the most respected universities in the region.

Our mission is to provide quality education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to succeed in their chosen fields and contribute meaningfully to society.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a diverse student body, RHIBMS BUEA offers a stimulating learning environment that fosters intellectual growth and personal development.

OUR PROGRAMS

Biomedicals

Our Biomedical programs prepare students for leadership roles in various hospital sectors. The curriculum covers biomedical principles, practicals, and events.

Duration: 4 years

Degree: Bachelor of Science

Management Department

The Management program provides comprehensive training in transport logistics, banking and finance, hotel management, and home economics.

Duration: 4 years

Degree: Higher National Diploma

Midwifery

Our Midwifery program addresses contemporary health challenges. Students learn about epidemiology, health policy, disease prevention, operations, delivery and community health promotion.

Duration: 4 years

Degree: Bachelor of Science

Engineering

The Engineering program offers specializations in Software, Civil, Electrical, and Mechanical Engineering. Students engage in practical projects and research to solve real-world problems.

Duration: 2 years

Degree: Higher National Diploma
